Caramel Popcorn Recipe

Delicious caramel popcorn perfect for any holiday or occasion.

Ingredients

  • 5 quarts popped popcorn
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Place popcorn in a very large bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in brown sugar and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil without stirring 4 minutes.
  3. Remove pan from heat and stir in baking soda. Pour caramel in a thin stream over popcorn while stirring. Stir until evenly coated.
  4. Divide popcorn between two large shallow baking dishes. Bake in the preheated oven, stirring every 15 minutes, for 1 hour.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool completely before breaking into pieces.
